Creating a Meme

  1. Pick an idea or joke

    Decide what reaction, punchline, or commentary you want. Relatable emotions and short, specific concepts work best.

  2. Choose a format or template

    Select an image or short video template. Recognizable formats often go more viral.

  3. Write concise text

    Use short, punchy captions. Keep wording simple and scannable.

  4. Design & layout

    Place text over or under the image. Use readable fonts, good contrast, and avoid clutter.

  5. Edit & resize

    Crop/resize for Instagram, Twitter, Discord, etc. Save a high-quality PNG/JPEG.
