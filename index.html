Creating a Meme

  1. Pick an idea or joke: Decide what reaction, punchline, or commentary you wantâ€”relatable emotions and short, specific concepts work best.

  2. Choose a format or template: Select an image or short video (classic template, reaction shot, screenshot, or your own photo). Recognizable templates help virality.

  3. Write concise text: Add a short, punchy caption. Use line breaks for timing. Keep wording simple and easy to read at a glance.

  4. Design & layout: Place text over or under the image using readable font (white text + thin dark outline or drop shadow is common). Maintain contrast and avoid clutter.

  5. Edit & resize: Crop and resize for your platform (Instagram, Twitter, Discord). Save a high-quality PNG/JPEG.
